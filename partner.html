How does it work?

Contact us to let us know if you want to rent or sell your machinery. We will call you to arrange a meeting to discuss details, resolve any doubts, and clarify important points. If you agree, we will add you as a supplier in our network.

What do you gain?

Clients: We will contact you when there are interested tenants or buyers, so you don't have to search for them.

Control: You decide the price, quantity, and type of machines to rent or sell.

Data: Our machine software provides valuable real-time information about the status of your equipment.

What do we gain?

We receive a percentage of the profit from each rental or sale, but you do not pay this directly. We aim to bring solutions to the industrial sector across the nation. We are expanding our presence and with your support, we will continue to grow.
